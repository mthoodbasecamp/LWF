The Mt. Hood Snosport Swap is the region's best winter sports equipment and clothing event featuring thousands of new and used winter sports items being sold by both individuals and area sports stores. Equipment, clothing and accessories for all winter sports are available, including nordic and alpine skiing, and snowboarding for beginners and experts. Sporting Goods shops from around the northwest will be there to sell thousands of new and demo’d equipment items at steep discounts!

Admission is $3 at the door, kids under 12 get in free, or bring the whole family for just $6.

Come early to beat the crowd! Door prizes all day long. Win gear and lift tickets.

Cash or credit cards accepted. No checks. ATM on site.

Frequently Asked Questions

If I want to sell some gear, what do I do?

Bring your equipment to sell to Washington Country Fairgrounds between Thursday 4 - 8pm, Friday 1pm - 9pm, or Saturday 9am - Noon Volunteers from the Mt. Hood Ski Patrol and the West Linn Snowboard Team will be on hand as you register your equipment and can provide you with valuation guidance.

All winter sports items are welcome: skis, snowboards, boots, clothing, etc. Please put clothing on plastic hangers. While all SnoSport Swap items are sold “as is” all items received for consignment are expected to be clean and in fully operational condition.

  • Skis & Snowboards: No equipment that is bent, delaminated, broken or has missing edge segments will be accepted.
  • No straight skis: This generally means nothing produced before 1998 or over 200 cm in length, with the exception of Nordic touring skis.
  • Bindings: Must be fully functional, conforming to current DIN/ISO standards for releasable & non-releasable (tele, nordic & snowboard) bindings, with no missing or damaged parts.
  • Boots: Must conform to current DIN/ISO standards for sole dimensions (must not be excessively worn). Buckle/lace systems should be entirely operational.
  • Clothing and Accessories: Should be in serviceable condition, with no broken zippers, tears, blown seams, missing closures, cracked lenses, etc.

Mt Hood Snosport Swap Management will have sole authority to determine whether items are acceptable for consignment.

Fees: $1.00 per item, plus 20% of the sale price when it sells. Outright donations are also accepted at no charge!

To collect your unsold gear, come back to the swap between 10:00 AM and 2:00 PM on Sunday, September 23rd. A check for gear that sold will be mailed to you the following week.

Why should I support the swap?

Money generated by the SWAP supports both the West Linn Snowboard Team and the Mt. Hood Ski Patrol.

The WLHS Snowboard Team is a school-affiliated sports club and a BSA Venturing Crew. Proceeds from the SWAP will help the Team members afford coaching, resort passes and transportation for their upcoming season.

The Mt. Hood Ski Patrol is a non-profit organization composed of nearly 300 volunteers, all dedicated to providing quality rescue and emergency care to participants in seasonal activities on Mt. Hood. Our patrol serves alpine and nordic skiers, and snowboarders at Mt. Hood Skibowl, Mt. Hood Meadows, Timberline, and Summit Ski Area. http://www.mthoodskipatrol.org
